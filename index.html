About Malabar Indoor Park

Kalmunai's Premier Sports Venue

Purpose-built for athletes and enthusiasts — play year-round regardless of weather.

Malabar Indoor Park is Kalmunai's most advanced indoor sports facility, offering professional courts for cricket, football, and badminton. Our fully covered, climate-controlled arena means weather never stops your game.

From casual weekend matches to competitive training sessions, we cater to all skill levels. Courts 1–3 feature full-size cricket pitches and 5-a-side football courts, while Court 4 is a dedicated BWF-standard badminton court.

Professional-grade synthetic turf
LED flood lighting throughout
Fully air-ventilated indoor
Secure changing facilities
Open 8 AM – 1 AM daily
Online booking system
Ample parking space
No spectator limits

Rules & Policies

Please read and respect all rules to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Proper Sports ShoesMandatory on all courts. No slippers, sandals, or bare feet allowed.
🧹
Maintain CleanlinessNo food or drinks on courts. Use waste bins. Report spills immediately.
⏰
Respect Time SlotsSessions begin at booked times. No extensions if the next slot is reserved.
⚽
Equipment ResponsibilityFootballs provided for Courts 1–3. All cricket gear must be brought by players.
🚭
No Smoking or AlcoholStrictly prohibited throughout the facility. Violations result in immediate removal.

Court 4 — Badminton OnlyBring Your Own Rackets and shuttles.
